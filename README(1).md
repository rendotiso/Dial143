# Dial 143
Dial 143 is a Dating-Sim built in Java. 
This game is set at a call center (Khai G. Call Center Agency), where the player is a newly hired agent who takes calls and tries to make a good impression on their newfound crush—the team leader. Earn salary, make friends, flirt with your crush, and unlock endings!

# Features
1. Immersive Call Center Setting
2. Multiple Love Interests (2 Men and 2 Women)
3. Different Endings for each route
4. Save/Load system using SQL

# Love Interests
1. Amaya - Your Childhood friend who happened to be now your fellow coworker!
2. Rosario - Your strict Team Leader with a hidden adorable side!
3. Cloma - Your HR senior whose sweet and warmth side is known to everyone!
4. Celeres - Your IT manager whose aloof nature brings comfort just only for you!
